About NUMA

NUMA (Nurses Medical Application) is a product of NurseBridge Network, Nigeria.

We are passionate about the quality of care nurses provide and the quality of life they live. We want to empower nurses - because we are nurses. We organize events, programs, and classes tailored to Nigerian and African nurses, and we build digital tools to modernize nursing practice.

Powered by the AFIX (Africa Future Innovation eXchange) initiative, which also powers other AFIX brands targeted at improving quality of life in Africa.

✓
Comprehensive exam preparation for Nigerian and international certifications
